About Me

My name is Ventum, and I am currently living in my favorite city: Kigali! I’m originally from Rwanda, Gisenyi, where the majority of my days were spent running track and driving to the Bralirwa to buy Primus. I then moved to Butare/Huye where I spent four arduous years at National University of Rwanda before graduating in 2012. With my diploma in a vice grip, I set out to explore what one of my friends once called “Iwabo w'abasalo”- which is apparently the capital of Rwanda.

This blog is my excuse to get to know people. It is a chance for me to get exposure. Most importantly, it is an opportunity for you and I to discover Kigali together.

Here’s to the journey,
